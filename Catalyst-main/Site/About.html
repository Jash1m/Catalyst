About Us

What is Catalyst?

Catalyst is a service that helps people improve their technical skills along with resume and job matching help. We started as a group of 6 devs in college that were worried about finding jobs after college. So we created a service to not only help recent graduates but anyone that wants to move up in the professional computer science world. Our website provides courses in many different computer science topics from coding in Python to using various libraries to build more complex programs. Certificates are given for each course completed. The certificates are automatically added to your resume after you upload it. In addition, we provide a service that takes your resume and certifications and matches you to jobs on Indeed and GlassDoor that the user would qualify for.
